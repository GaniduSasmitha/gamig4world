​The COLORFUL CVN B650 FROZEN V14 is a stylish ATX motherboard tailored for AMD Ryzen 7000 and 8000 series processors, featuring a 16+2+1 phase power design with 55A DrMOS for robust performance. It supports up to 192GB of DDR5 memory at speeds up to 7600MHz (OC) and offers dual PCIe 5.0 x4 M.2 slots for high-speed storage. Connectivity includes Realtek 2.5GbE LAN, Intel Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.1, USB 3.2 Gen 2 Type-C, and HDMI 2.0 and DisplayPort 1.2 outputs supporting up to 4K@60Hz resolutions. The motherboard's white camo design with RGB lighting, controllable via iGame Center software, makes it an appealing choice for gamers and PC enthusiasts seeking both performance and aesthetics.
