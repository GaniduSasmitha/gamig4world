​The Samsung 980 NVMe M.2 SSD 500GB is a high-performance storage solution designed for users seeking fast and reliable data access. Utilizing a PCIe 3.0 x4 interface and NVMe 1.4 protocol, it delivers sequential read speeds up to 3,100 MB/s and write speeds up to 2,600 MB/s . Built with Samsung's V-NAND 3-bit MLC flash and an in-house controller, this SSD ensures efficient performance without a DRAM cache, instead employing Host Memory Buffer (HMB) technology to maintain speed and responsiveness . Its M.2 2280 form factor makes it suitable for a wide range of devices, including desktops, laptops, and gaming systems
