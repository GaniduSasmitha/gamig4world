​The MSI GeForce RTX 5080 16G SHADOW 3X OC is a high-performance graphics card built on NVIDIA's Blackwell architecture, featuring 16GB of GDDR7 memory and 10,752 CUDA cores. It offers a boost clock of up to 2640 MHz and utilizes a triple-fan TORX Fan 5.0 cooling system with a nickel-plated copper baseplate and square-shaped core pipes for efficient heat dissipation. The card supports PCIe 5.0, includes three DisplayPort 2.1b ports and one HDMI 2.1b port, and is capable of delivering up to 8K resolution at 120Hz with HDR and VRR. With a power consumption of 360W, MSI recommends an 850W power supply for optimal performance. The SHADOW 3X OC is designed for gamers and creators seeking top-tier graphics performance in a sleek and efficient package.
